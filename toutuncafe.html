Tout un café

Tou

Tout un cafe is a showcase of an innovative collective of talented French Artists in painting and illustrating. This website helps them to show their creations to anyone, specialists and non specialist in a fancy and an immersive way.

It is imagined as a central "platform" for any followers who wish to stay connected to their favorite artists. They can take a look on latest painting galleries, biography, next event or upcoming expositions.

Website Sections
01
Welcome Home

From the conception, I decided to present the collective as a platform of content about painting and artist life like their lastest news, biography, interview, social games and so on. I tried to break the classic grid to put it as webzine style and also put a fixed black side menu to have access to another section without scrolling.

02
Expositions

This section helps users to have an overview on the latest and further exposition of the collective in Paris. There's an poster for each exposition with complementary information like : the date, the opening hours and location. You can also book a ticket in advance directly from the website.

03
Artists Biography

Each Artist has a nice presentation helped by an immersive profile background picture with a light blur on it. At the bottom you have an access to contact info to let recruiter call artists if they like their creation. On the right side, there are all the paintings from the artist and by clicking on the arrow button you'll have Fullscreen visualisation of it.

04
Media center

This section is a strong link between Artists and the community of people who attended expositions or any event. After each event, user can see pictures or even a video of it which are organized and presented in an authentic way.
